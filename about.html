About this editor

HTML Editor by Jignesh is a free, client-side tool for writing HTML with a Monaco code editor, live preview, optional visual editing, templates, and local snapshots. No account is required; your work can stay in your browser until you export or share it.

You can switch between Code, Visual, and Split modes, use built-in starter templates (hero, blog, pricing, and more), compare versions with snapshot diff, and generate share links or QR codes—all without sending your HTML to a server for editing.

Open the app from the home page to start editing.
